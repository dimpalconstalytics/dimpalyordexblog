Supplier Portal

Shared Orders

Smart PO’s are available online or by API from requisition to final payment so there is always a “single version of the truth” between you and your supplier.

So why does that matter? By having a single version of the truth, we can automatically verify invoices and other documents and send them back to the supplier if they are not correct. We can even generate the invoices ourselves.

Order Tracking

Smart PO’s don’t stop when the PO is sent. They stay alive until the final payment is confirmed so all parties always get a real-time view of the delivery, invoice approval and payment status.

Smart Terms

Smart Terms not only describe the terms, but also make sure those terms are adhered to. Using Smart Terms, we know who needs to do what when so we can guide employees and suppliers through the correct process every time. This saves you time chasing people and gives you confidence processes are adhered to without requiring constant training.

Automated Alerting

The Smart Terms also keep track of due dates for every process step. When actions do not happen on time, we will automatically alert the responsible people to remind them. This helps make sure orders never get stuck.

Messaging

Our PO’s are smart, but they can’t resolve everything. Some issues can only be resolved by people. Every order has its own messaging channel allowing you, your supplier and anyone else in your company to resolve those issues. Centralised communications means you won’t have to trawl through lots of emails to get a status update.

Adaptive Format

Different suppliers may want to receive the PO in different formats. Yordex will translate the PO to the format required by your suppliers, meaning less change for everyone involved.

Custom Forms

Custom Forms allow you to add additional data fields to our PO or supplier forms. This will help you keep your business running smoothly by making sure we always capture the data you need.

Invoice Central

With a Supplier Portal in place, invoicing becomes a breeze. Yordex supports a range of invoicing options to suit every supplier.

  • Supplier Self Matching

    With this option your supplier uploads the invoice on the portal and matches it with the PO. They are asked to confirm the amount and payment due date matches with what we have on file. If not, they either change the invoice, or request a change to the order. If they request a change, the responsible employees have to approve that first. This avoids you ever getting invoices that don't match.
  • Self Billing

    In this option, we generate the invoice based on the original order and a confirmation of what was received. Unlike other self-billing systems, no special agreement is required for tax purposes. Because the supplier has an account on our portal, we still generate the invoice on behalf of the supplier.
  • Invoice Scanning

    The easiest option for your suppliers is just continue to send you the invoice as they do today. Yordex will scan it and match it with your PO’s.

PO Approvals

When using this module, Yordex will route the PO to all required people for approval.

Routing is based on our flexible PO approval rules which you choose based on the PO amount, spend category or cost centres. Approval of the PO can be done anytime, anywhere by email or from our mobile app.

Payments

With Smart POs in place, automating payments becomes a breeze. Yordex offers a range of payment options depending on your needs.

Auto Pay

With Auto Pay, payment details are put automatically onto your bank feed. Payments are then made using your existing payment provider and process.

FX Pay

Through our partnership with Transferwise, Yordex FX Payments lowers your payments costs and makes sure the money arrives with your suppliers faster.

Mass Pay

With Yordex Mass Pay you make one periodic payment to Yordex and we will take care of paying your individual suppliers, saving time and lowering payment costs.

Supplier Discounts

Improving use of early payment discounts and supply chain finance can lead to 0.2% to 0.5% savings on working capital. That is £200K to £500K savings on every £100M spend straight to your bottom line.

We automate the process of maximising supplier discounts through a number of options.

  • Early Payment Discounts
  • Dynamic Discounting
  • Supply Chain Finance

Early Payment Discounts

Smart PO’s can keep track of early payment discounts and help you make sure you capture those discounts wherever possible.

Dynamic Discounting

Dynamic Discounting is also called “discounting for the 21st century”. Your supplier can offer discounts only when they need it while you can negotiate better payment terms by offering this option.

Supply Chain Finance

Extend Dynamic Discounting with a third party Supply Chain Finance provider. Take advantage of supplier discounts even if you don’t have the cash on hand to pay early. Bring your own Supply Chain Finance provider or use one of our partners.

Reporting

Spend Reports

Spend Analytics

Analytics service that allows you to use wealth of data generated by Smart PO’s to continuously identify new opportunities to improve process efficiency, cash flow or payment terms.
